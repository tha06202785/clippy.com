## 2024-05-22 - [AI Workflow Portability]
**Learning:** In AI-centric workflows where "Human-in-the-loop" review is mandatory, users often need to export or repurpose generated content beyond the immediate interface. Providing a one-click "Copy" action directly next to the AI output significantly reduces friction for users who want to save drafts or use the content in external marketing materials.
**Action:** Always include easy export or copy actions for AI-generated text blocks to respect the user's broader workflow.
