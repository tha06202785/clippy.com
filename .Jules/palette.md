## 2025-05-14 - AI Description Clipboard Copy
**Learning:** Providing a 'Copy to clipboard' action for AI-generated text significantly improves user workflow by allowing them to easily move content to other platforms (e.g., social media ads, MLS listings).
**Action:** Always include a Copy button for long-form AI content, ensuring clear visual feedback ('Copied!') and accessibility markers (`aria-live`, `aria-label`).
