## 2024-06-25 - [AI Copy to Clipboard Pattern]
**Learning:** Providing a "Copy to clipboard" action for AI-generated text is a critical UX pattern. It acknowledges that AI content is often a starting point that users may want to port to other tools, and provides immediate feedback (e.g., "Copied!") to confirm success.
**Action:** Always include a prominent copy-to-clipboard button with visual feedback for large blocks of AI-generated content.
